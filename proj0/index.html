Why?

I think the face distortion when you take a close up selfie happens because the nose (and face in general) is too close to the camera lens, making the face look elongated and more fish-like. On the other hand, when the camera is positioned a few feet from the face, the perspective is more true to how we usually see each other in real life, making the face look more "natural".
